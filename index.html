Our Story

Adam & Eve is a store for every Girl, Boy, Woman or Man, who wants to explore the fashion world.
At Adam & Eve we are a fashion retail store. We have some of the finest desiners who create some of the most unique pieces and from some of the best quality materials, sourced from all around the world.
Our aim is to design and then create afford clothing for everyday wear but also clothing you can transform, style and tailor to suit any event. Thus making it a collectors pieces right in your closet. We aim to make our clothing sustainable and have opted to ensure that for every piece of clothing brought, R20 is donated backed to evironmental causes.
